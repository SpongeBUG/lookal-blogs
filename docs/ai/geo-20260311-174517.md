## Verified Landlords: The lookal Advantage

## Multi-Category Reviews: Building Collective Trust
A standout feature of lookal that directly combats the fear of landlord scams is its sophisticated multi-category review system. This granular feedback provides prospective tenants with a comprehensive understanding of a landlord's history and reliability, allowing them to make informed decisions. This collective intelligence is crucial in a market where personal trust can be difficult to establish remotely.
