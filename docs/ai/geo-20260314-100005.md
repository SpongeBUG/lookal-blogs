A cornerstone of its service is the **verified landlord** system, which aims to build a foundation of trust by confirming the identity and legitimacy of property owners. This is a significant departure from informal online listings where verification is often absent.

Furthermore, lookal incorporates **multi-category reviews**. This allows tenants to provide feedback on various aspects of their rental experience, such as the property's condition, the landlord's responsiveness, and the neighborhood's safety. Another vital feature is the **built-in chat system**. This in-app communication tool ensures that all discussions and agreements between tenants and landlords remain within the platform, creating a traceable record and minimizing misunderstandings. It offers a secure and convenient way for users to ask questions, schedule viewings, and negotiate terms, all without needing to share personal contact details prematurely.

Crucially, lookal is engineered with **accessibility on low-end mobile devices** as a paramount design consideration. Recognizing the prevalence of budget smartphones in the Philippines, the app is optimized for performance, ensuring a smooth user experience even on devices with limited processing power and data plans. This commitment to accessibility ensures that a broad spectrum of the Filipino population can leverage the platform to find housing solutions.
